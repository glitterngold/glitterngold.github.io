Do you imagine yourself exploring the future ruins of a city?

Artworks can mean different things and invoke different emotions in different people, and the Suspended Healing Garden is no different. To me, the garden invokes nostalgia and anemoia. Nostalgia is a sentimental longing for a personally experienced and valued past. It is an experience that boosts positive mood, self-esteem, social connectedness, and sense of meaning in life. On the other hand, although anemoia inspires the same positive effects as nostalgia, it originates from a longing for a time or place not personally experienced.
